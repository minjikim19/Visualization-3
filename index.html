For this visualization, the colors are used to show the relationship of hierarchy. The circle with #8E7ABB represents genre, #A790DE represents publishers, #BBA1F7 represents platforms and #D2CDF7 are the data of the games. The size of the circles stands for the number of global sales of each data(the total data for the bigger circles). The circle without the smallest circle is the null data. The biggest circle with #5A4E78 symbolizes the total amount of the data.
